01 / What We Measure

A complete picture
of cardiovascular risk

01

Resting ECG

12-lead electrocardiogram capturing electrical activity at rest, analysed on-site with immediate interpretation.

02

Blood Pressure Profile

Multi-reading protocol across both arms. Ambulatory patterns and pulse pressure analysis.

03

Arterial Stiffness

Pulse wave velocity measurement — a key predictor of future cardiovascular events, rarely available outside hospital settings.

Partner Lab Network

Seamless referrals for lipid panels, inflammation markers, HbA1c and extended bloods through our accredited partner laboratories.
